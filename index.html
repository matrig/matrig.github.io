Mattia Rigotti · AI and Neuroscience Research

I am a Senior Research Scientist at IBM Research AI, working at the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience. My research covers reasoning in LLMs, visual foundation models, trustworthy AI, and AI for science, as well as the geometry of neural representations in the brain.
