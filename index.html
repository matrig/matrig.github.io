Mattia Rigotti — Machine Learning and Neuroscience Research

I am a Senior Research Scientist at IBM Research AI, working at the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience. My research covers reasoning in LLMs, visual foundation models, and the geometry of neural representations in the brain.
